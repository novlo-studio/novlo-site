Our Story

Built On
Good Times
& Bad Decisions.

The Anchor has stood on this corner for over three decades. Not because we chased trends — but because we never had to. From the first pint to the last call, we've been the place locals come back to.

We believe a great pub isn't designed — it's earned. Rough edges, regulars who know your name, and a kitchen that doesn't quit when you need it most.
