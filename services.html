FAQ

Common
questions

Anything not covered here — ask directly. I respond within one business day.

  • 01
    How long does each project take?

    Starter sites are typically built within a few focused working days once content and scope are finalized. Business sites take 1–3 weeks. Immersive projects take 3–5 weeks depending on the complexity of the 3D build.

  • 02
    What counts as a "revision"?

    A revision is a round of changes — not individual tweaks. Each tier includes 2 revision rounds. Additional rounds are billed at CA$100/hour, or we can discuss a custom package.

  • 03
    Can I upgrade my site later?

    Absolutely. Many clients start with Starter and expand to multi-page as their business grows. The code is clean, scalable, and built without technical debt from day one.

  • 04
    Do you write the content?

    I can recommend copywriters, or we can work with your existing content. For AI Visuals, I handle everything from prompt to final image delivery.

  • 05
    What if I need more than 2 updates per year?

    Additional updates are billed at CA$100/hr, or we can set up a custom care plan that fits your actual needs.
